# X / Twitter

## Purpose

Short public updates and threads.

## Audience

- builders who scan quickly and reward sharp framing
- people discovering Punk through one clear lesson or tension
- followers of AI tooling, agent workflows, and public-build projects

## Shared style source

Inherit the shared voice and policy from:

- `public/channels/_shared/style.toml`
- `public/channels/_shared/voice.md`

## Format rules

- use a sharp first line with one concrete tension, lesson, or claim
- keep one idea per post; split into a thread when needed
- avoid hashtags by default
- use at most one link, usually at the end
- prefer short sentences and short line breaks
- when mentioning the product directly, keep the maturity honest: experimental, early-stage, not production-ready
- do not imply that parked modules or automation already exist

## Manual publication steps

1. Finalize the channel variant in `public/posts/`.
2. Publish manually.
3. Record publication receipt in `public/publications/`.
4. Add metrics snapshot manually when useful.

## Metrics to collect

- impressions
- likes
- replies
- reposts
- follows
