# Telegram

## Purpose

Short updates and community notes.

## Audience

- readers following the build more closely than on fast social timelines
- technical community members who want compact but concrete updates
- early supporters interested in experiments, failures, and direction changes

## Shared style source

Inherit the shared voice and policy from:

- `public/channels/_shared/style.toml`
- `public/channels/_shared/voice.md`

## Format rules

- lead with a value sentence or lesson, not with a title line
- keep the post compact; prefer one main idea and one supporting example
- one link max unless a second link is essential
- no hashtags by default
- use short paragraphs; bullets are allowed only when they improve scanning
- slightly more personal than the blog, but still evidence-first and concrete
- keep active versus parked capabilities explicit when discussing product status

## Manual publication steps

1. Finalize the channel variant in `public/posts/`.
2. Publish manually.
3. Record publication receipt in `public/publications/`.
4. Add metrics snapshot manually when useful.

## Metrics to collect

- views
- reactions
- comments
- shares
- subscribers
